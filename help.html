Using GradeGrub

Individual Assignments

  • Click on your score or the point value of an assignment to change its value
  • Click on the blank space on an assignment that hasn't been entered yet to give it a value
  • Click the blue arrow to revert a changed grade back to its original value
  • Add a new assignment by clicking the "new assignment" button
  • Adjust the name, score, and maximum point value of a new assignment by clicking on them. Change the category by selecting from the drop-down menu.

Automatic adjustment

Automatic adjustment sets a group of grades to all take on the same percentage value in order to match a particular target.

  • Click your overall class grade to automatically adjust a group of grades to match a target.
  • Select the grades to be adjusted with the checkboxes, and use the dropdown menu or the textbox to choose a target grade.
  • Change the degree of precision with the dropdown menu.
